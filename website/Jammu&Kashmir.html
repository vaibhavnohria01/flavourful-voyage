Welcome to Jammu & Kashmir, where majestic mountains, serene lakes, rich culture, and warm hospitality converge to create an unforgettable experience. Discover the timeless allure of the Kashmir Valley, where verdant meadows, azure skies, and snow-capped peaks paint a picture-perfect landscape. Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Jammu & Kashmir, where centuries-old traditions are celebrated with fervor, from colorful festivals to intricate handicrafts like Pashmina shawls and Kashmiri carpets. Savor the flavors of Kashmiri cuisine, with its aromatic spices and rich flavors, from mouthwatering Rogan Josh to the delicate sweetness of Kashmiri pulao. Explore the spiritual heritage of the region, with its ancient temples, mosques, and shrines that offer solace and inspiration amidst the natural beauty. Whether you're cruising on Dal Lake, trekking in the Himalayas, or simply sipping kahwa by a crackling fire, Jammu & Kashmir promises an experience that will stay with you long after you've left its breathtaking landscapes.



My favourite food: "Kashmiri Pulao"

A place must to visit: "Dal Lake"

Best time to visit: May to November


Fun Fact: One lesser-known fact about Jammu and Kashmir is its association with the Hemis Festival, one of the most vibrant and significant Buddhist festivals celebrated in the region.
