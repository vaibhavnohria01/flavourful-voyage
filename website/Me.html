About the Author!!

Hello! I'm Vaibhav Nohria, a Master of Computing student at ANU. Originally from Delhi, India, I'm passionate about technology and exploration. When not diving into tech, I enjoy table tennis matches, diving into captivating books, and losing myself in the melodies of the violin. Soccer and music are constant companions in my life. India's diverse cultures fascinate me, from the bustling streets of Delhi to the serene backwaters of Kerala. Old Delhi's historical charm and vibrant mix of languages and traditions captivate the senses. Nature is my sanctuary, and hiking through its wonders is how I find peace and inspiration. Travel isn't just a hobby; it's an integral part of who I am. Exploring new cultures, savouring diverse cuisines, and discovering the beauty of different landscapes invigorate me. I'm always looking for the latest tech trends, eager to push boundaries and embrace innovation. Join me on this journey of discovery and growth!
