Fun Fact: One lesser-known fact about Ladakh is its association with ancient trade routes that facilitated cultural exchange and commerce between Central Asia, Tibet, and India. While Ladakh is often celebrated for its breathtaking landscapes and Buddhist monasteries, its historical significance as a hub for the Silk Road and other trade routes is less widely recognized.
