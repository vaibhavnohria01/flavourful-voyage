Fun Fact: One lesser-known fact about Haryana is its historical significance as the birthplace of the ancient Indian epic, the Bhagavad Gita. While the Bhagavad Gita is widely revered and studied across the world, fewer may know that it was spoken on the battlefield of Kurukshetra in present-day Haryana.
