Experience the vibrant tapestry of Andhra Pradesh! Indulge your senses in a journey through its rich culture, tantalizing food, cherished traditions, timeless beauty, and breathtaking nature. From the aromatic spices of its cuisine to the rhythmic beats of its classical dance forms, Andhra Pradesh invites you to immerse yourself in its colorful heritage. Explore ancient temples, lush green landscapes, and pristine beaches that showcase the state's natural splendor. Discover the essence of Andhra Pradesh – where every moment is a celebration of life's abundance.

Best time to visit: December to February


Fun Fact: While many people may be aware of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and its achievements, fewer may know that Andhra Pradesh hosts one of the most prominent space research centers in the world – the Satish Dhawan Space Centre (SDSC) or Sriharikota Range (SHAR).
